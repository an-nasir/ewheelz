# Growth Playbook

## Ruthless Positioning

Use this line:

> eWheelz checks used EV deals in Pakistan and creates verified WhatsApp buyer-seller intros.

Do not pitch "another EV marketplace." Sellers already have PakWheels, OLX, and Facebook. The wedge is narrower:

- Buyer asks: "Is this EV deal safe?"
- eWheelz checks price, battery risk, source quality, and seller contact.
- eWheelz introduces serious buyers to sellers on WhatsApp.

## What Counts

Investor-grade proof is not traffic, posts, or scraped inventory.

Count only:

- Verified direct listing: seller/dealer confirms WhatsApp, price, city, photos, and permission to list.
- Qualified buyer request: buyer gives phone, budget, city, preferred model, and buying timeline.
- WhatsApp intro: buyer and seller are connected or both explicitly receive the same listing context.
- Weekly inventory commitment: seller/dealer agrees to send updated stock at least once per week.

Do not count:

- Source-only scraped listings.
- Generic pageviews.
- Likes, impressions, or comments without DM/WhatsApp follow-up.
- "Interested" buyers without budget and phone.
- Sellers who only say "send details" but never provide inventory.

## 7-Day Target

Realistic first proof target:

- 10 verified direct listings.
- 10 qualified buyer requests.
- 5 logged buyer-seller WhatsApp intros.
- 3 sellers/dealers committed to weekly inventory updates.
- 1 public market scorecard with sample size and caveats.

Stretch target:

- 20 verified direct listings.
- 10 intros.
- 1 seller/dealer asks for priority placement.

## Daily Operating Rules

- Work one tight wedge first: Lahore and Islamabad; BYD Atto 3, MG ZS EV, Deepal S07, BYD Seal.
- Message sellers before posting content.
- Message buyers from deal-check hooks before polishing pages.
- Every conversation must end in one of: listing added, buyer request captured, intro made, follow-up dated, or disqualified.
- Source-only listings are allowed for market context, but they do not count as supply.

## Tracker Columns

Use a spreadsheet with these columns:

```text
date,channel,contact_name,type,city,phone_or_url,vehicle,price_pkr,status,verification_status,buyer_budget,buyer_timeline,intro_status,next_follow_up,listing_id,notes
```

Statuses:

- `contacted`
- `replied`
- `qualified`
- `listing_verified`
- `buyer_request_captured`
- `intro_made`
- `weekly_inventory_committed`
- `follow_up_needed`
- `not_interested`
- `bad_fit`

Verification statuses:

- `source_only`
- `phone_confirmed`
- `seller_confirmed`
- `photos_confirmed`
- `stale`

## Day 0 - Before The Sprint

- [ ] Create the tracker.
- [ ] Prepare one WhatsApp broadcast/contact list for buyers.
- [ ] Prepare one seller/dealer list with at least 60 prospects.
- [ ] Decide exact wedge: city plus 3-4 EV models.
- [ ] Confirm site shows source-only and direct-contact labels correctly.
- [ ] Confirm admin can approve without seller verification.
- [ ] Confirm "seller verified" is used only after manual contact.

## Day 1 - Seller Reality Check

Goal: prove sellers will cooperate.

- [ ] Contact 30 sellers/dealers from PakWheels, OLX, Facebook, and WhatsApp groups.
- [ ] Ask only for: model/year, price, city, WhatsApp, photos, permission to list.
- [ ] Verify and publish 2 direct listings.
- [ ] Log every refusal reason.
- [ ] Public post: "Send me any EV ad. I will tell you price risk and battery questions to ask."

Pass condition:

- 2 verified direct listings or 5 sellers willing to share inventory.

Fail signal:

- Sellers ignore you because the pitch is "free listing." Change pitch to buyer leads.

## Day 2 - Buyer Demand Test

Goal: prove buyers want deal-check help.

- [ ] Run 10 manual deal checks from real ads.
- [ ] DM/comment back with one useful red flag, not a sales pitch.
- [ ] Capture 3 qualified buyer requests.
- [ ] Verify and publish 2 more direct listings.
- [ ] Start a buyer-alert WhatsApp list manually.

Pass condition:

- 3 buyers give budget/city/timeline/phone.

Fail signal:

- Buyers like posts but do not DM. Make the hook more concrete: "Drop a link, I will say overpriced/fair/risky."

## Day 3 - First Intros

Goal: make the marketplace happen manually.

- [ ] Match buyer requests to verified direct listings.
- [ ] Make 1-2 WhatsApp intros.
- [ ] Ask sellers if the buyer was relevant.
- [ ] Ask buyers what blocked purchase.
- [ ] Verify and publish 2 more direct listings.

Pass condition:

- At least 1 real buyer-seller intro.

Fail signal:

- Buyers are browsing, not buying. Add timeline filter and stop chasing vague leads.

## Day 4 - Dealer Commitment

Goal: move from one-off listings to recurring supply.

- [ ] Identify top 5 responsive sellers/dealers.
- [ ] Offer founding seller placement only if they send weekly updated inventory.
- [ ] Get 1 weekly inventory commitment.
- [ ] Publish one small inventory drop: 3-5 verified direct listings or clearly labeled source-only picks.
- [ ] Capture 2 more buyer requests.

Pass condition:

- 1 weekly inventory commitment and 1 more intro.

Fail signal:

- Dealers ask "how many leads?" Show exact buyer requests, not traffic.

## Day 5 - Trust Content That Sells

Goal: make content produce conversations, not vanity.

- [ ] Publish one used EV buyer checklist.
- [ ] Publish one battery-risk red flag post.
- [ ] Publish one price-risk example with confidence label.
- [ ] DM every engager with: "Send your budget/city/model and I will match listings."
- [ ] Verify and publish 2 more direct listings.

Pass condition:

- 2 qualified buyer requests from content.

Fail signal:

- Content gets likes but no requests. Stop broad education and return to deal-check hooks.

## Day 6 - Close The Loop

Goal: prove seller value.

- [ ] Make 2 more WhatsApp intros.
- [ ] Send sellers screenshots or summaries of buyer interest.
- [ ] Ask 5 sellers/dealers for weekly stock update permission.
- [ ] Get total verified direct listings to 10.
- [ ] Draft the scorecard with honest sample sizes.

Pass condition:

- 5 total intros or a clear reason why matching failed.

Fail signal:

- You have listings but no qualified buyers. Shift next week to buyer acquisition before seller acquisition.

## Day 7 - Public Proof

Goal: publish proof that cannot be faked.

- [ ] Publish the 7-day scorecard.
- [ ] Include verified direct listings, source-only listings, buyer requests, intros, and top red flags.
- [ ] DM scorecard to every responsive seller/dealer.
- [ ] Ask for weekly inventory from the top 5.
- [ ] Decide next week's wedge based on conversion, not preference.

Scorecard format:

```text
eWheelz 7-day used EV market check

Verified direct listings: [number]
Source-only listings tracked: [number]
Qualified buyer requests: [number]
Buyer-seller WhatsApp intros: [number]
Cities covered: [cities]
Most requested models: [models]
Most common red flag: [red flag]
Best performing channel: [channel]

Next week: verified dealer inventory for [city/model wedge].
```

```text
I will check:
1. Price risk
2. Battery questions to ask
3. Seller/source quality
4. What to verify before token

```

## Stop Doing

- Do not count scraped listings as supply.
- Do not call a seller verified unless contact was manually confirmed.
- Do not call battery output verified unless a real inspection happened.
- Do not post broad launch announcements.
- Do not build mobile, subscriptions, dashboards, or SEO extras during the sprint.
- Do not hide weak numbers. Weak numbers are useful if they show where the market is blocked.
